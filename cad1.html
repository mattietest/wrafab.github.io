ASSIGNMNET

You need to pick one of the following:

1. Using Fusion 360, design a wax seal stamp that will be 3D printed. You can see an example video of how you may create it here.

OR

2. Create your own lampshade using Fusion 360. We will 3D print the lampshade. You can see an example of how you may create it here or here.

OR

3. Create an enclosure for a 3D printed surround for a solder fume extractor using Fusion 360 and recycled computer fans. You can see an example of how you may create it here.

You must document your choice using the Class Page. You should include pictures and the Fusion 360 original files on your page. Illustrate what works and did not work when designing the enclosure. It is important to reflect on your success and failure.
