Digital Fabrication and Engineering

Course Description

This course introduces students to the principles and applications of digital fabrication. It is a hands-on course designed around rapid prototyping and discovery of digital fabrication tools. The course will specifically look at project management, design thinking, computer-aided design, computer controlled cutting, electronics production, 3D scanning and printing, electronics design, machining, molding and casting, input devices, output devices, composites, mechanical design, invention and intellectual property. Each student will complete a series of projects illustrating their competence in each category.
