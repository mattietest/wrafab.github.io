    Assignment

    1. Laminate your Skateboard using Titebond III glue.

    2.Vacuum bag your skateboard.

    3.Decorate your skateboard using the laser, vinyl cutter or vinyl printer. Coat your board with the SPAR Urethane.

    4.Take pictures of your skateboard when you are finished and add pictures of both the front and back and describe the process to create the skateboard on you webpage.
